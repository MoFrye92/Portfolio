VibeCheckr

A gentle space to check in with yourself. Reflect, discover music that resonates with your mood, and reset your vibe when you need it.

Start Your Vibe Check

Why It Helps

Checking in with yourself shouldn’t feel clinical. VibeCheckr invites you to pause, reflect, and shift your energy in small, meaningful ways.

Build Self‑Awareness

Take a moment to notice how you feel. Acknowledge your vibe and learn patterns over time.

Find Mood‑Based Music

Discover music that matches or changes your vibe (planned integration with streaming APIs).

Reset & Reflect

Write a quick note. Feel what needs feeling. Then enjoy a fresh vibe with music or a mood reset.

How It Works

In three simple steps, VibeCheckr guides you through acknowledging your feelings, capturing them, and shifting your energy.

1. Check Your Vibe

Choose a mood from our gentle list. Whether you’re feeling good, drained or somewhere in between, we’ve got you.

2. Journal Briefly

Write a short reflection about why you feel this way (or skip if you just want music right away).

3. Discover & Reset

Listen to a curated music recommendation and revisit past check‑ins in your personal vibe history.

Features

Mood Check‑In

Friendly mood options and a “journey” flow for deeper nuance. No clinical language here.

Journaling

A simple entry field to jot down your thoughts. Stored privately in your browser (no accounts needed).

Music Pairing

Planned integration with music APIs to match your vibe or offer a reset playlist.

Vibe Trends

Review your past check‑ins and notice patterns over time. This helps you understand what influences your mood.
